About BakeryKart

Remember the eagerness you observed as a kid when your birthday cake was carried into the room? We want to assist reproduce that same energy and experience for your friends, clients or loved ones' special day with our delectable gourmet food gifts.

BakeryKart! began in 2004 as the launch of our service for gourmet birthday cake delivery. Our cakes are made with only the finest, freshest ingredients by our award-winning Pune bakeries. There is no better way to celebrate a special occasion than to send a little gratification from someone who cares. Our Guarantee: On-Time Delivery and a Quality Gift

Cakes are the perfect option to all to make their bond stronger with loved ones, clients, and more. Our mission at Pune Bakers is to spread sweetness throughout the world. We want to enhance the relationships of our customers with their family, colleagues, and business partners.
